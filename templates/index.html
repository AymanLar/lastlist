Lastspot

Create Perfect Playlists Effortlessly!

Tired of scrolling through endless songs? Look no further! Lastspot helps you craft personalized Spotify playlists based on your Last.fm listening history. Whether you want to curate your own tunes or surprise friends with a thoughtfully designed playlist, our app has got you covered!

Why Use Lastspot ?

  • Personalized Playlists: Automatically generate playlists based on your unique Last.fm listening habits.
  • Surprise Friends: Create playlists for friends using their Last.fm accounts to introduce them to new music they'll love!
  • Explore New Genres: Discover tracks you might have missed, expanding your musical horizons.
  • Easy to Use: Our user-friendly interface makes playlist creation a breeze for everyone!

How to Use the App (It's simple!)

  1. Log in with Spotify: Start by logging in with your Spotify account.
  2. Enter a Last.fm Username: Enter the Last.fm username of the person whose listening history you'd like to base the playlist on.
  3. Check Your Spotify: The playlist will appear in your Spotify account, and you can enjoy your music instantly!
